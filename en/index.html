DESTINY TALES

An author project by Vitaliy Puzyryov

Some books don’t appear because someone decided to write them.
They appear because something has already happened —
and staying silent is no longer possible.

They are not planned.
They are not explained.
They are simply left — as they were written.

This is not a blog.
And not a store.

These are books assembled from lived experience.
Without trying to fix anything in hindsight.
Without trying to look better
than it really was.

There are things you understand too late.
There are decisions made without words.
There are pages you return to not for answers —
but to make sure it really happened.

Some things are not read right away.
You simply return to them.
